e-Mitra ज्ञान is an effort to bring the advantages of Artificial Intelligence to the masses of Rajasthan via the e-Mitra portal. It is designed to allow anyone to leverage Artificial Intelligence for two kinds of tasks:

  • Classification: Tasks which require putting things in one or many classes. For example: Does this photo contain a human face?(Yes and No being the classes)
  • Regression: Tasks which require producing a number. For example: How many grains of wheat are there in this photo?

The service provides two opportunities to the general populace. First there is the ability to train systems to recognize things for you. This has multiple applications like answering questions such as:

  • How much Iron is there in this picture of a soil sample?
  • Which pest is present in this photo?
  • What percent of this crop might die in the future?

In order to answer these questions an AI is trained on historic example. To generate data for this, people are employed on a contractual basis to correctly label the data currently available. This provides employment opportunity to the population.
